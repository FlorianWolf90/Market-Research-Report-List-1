<p><p>The Medical Grade Polyoxymethylene (POM) market is divided into two main types: Homopolymer POM and Copolymer POM. Homopolymer POM, known for its superior strength and stiffness, is commonly utilized in medical devices requiring durability and precision. In contrast, Copolymer POM offers better impact resistance and chemical stability, making it suitable for applications where flexibility and environmental stress are critical. Both types cater to the healthcare sector, ensuring compliance with stringent medical standards and enhancing product performance.</p></p>
