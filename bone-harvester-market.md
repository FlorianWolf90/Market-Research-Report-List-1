<p><strong>The Bone Harvester Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cancellous Bone Harvesting</li><li>Marrow Harvesting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Bone Harvester market encompasses two primary types: Cancellous Bone Harvesting and Marrow Harvesting. Cancellous Bone Harvesting focuses on extracting spongy bone tissue, often used in orthopedic and dental procedures for grafting and enhancing structural support. Marrow Harvesting, on the other hand, involves obtaining stem cells and hematopoietic cells from the bone marrow for therapeutic applications, including treatments for leukemia and regenerative medicine. Both types play vital roles in improving patient outcomes in various surgical contexts.</p></p>
