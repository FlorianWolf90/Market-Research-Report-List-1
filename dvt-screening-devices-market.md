<p><p>DVT screening devices encompass various market types, primarily categorized into imaging modalities, intravascular ultrasound, and others. Imaging modalities, such as Doppler ultrasound and MRI, provide non-invasive visualization of blood vessels to detect clots. Intravascular ultrasound (IVUS) involves inserting a probe into blood vessels to obtain detailed images from within, offering precise clot assessment. Other methods may include blood tests and photoplethysmography, which measure changes in blood volume. Together, these technologies enhance DVT diagnosis and management by improving detection accuracy.</p></p>
