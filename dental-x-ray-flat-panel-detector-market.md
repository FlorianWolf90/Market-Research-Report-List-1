<p><p>The Dental X-Ray Flat Panel Detector market is categorized into two main types: Direct Conversion and Indirect Conversion. </p><p>Direct Conversion detectors utilize materials that convert X-ray photons directly into electrical signals, resulting in high image quality and faster processing times. In contrast, Indirect Conversion detectors rely on a two-step process, where X-rays are first converted into light, which is then transformed into electrical signals. While Indirect Conversion systems may be more cost-effective, Direct Conversion provides superior image resolution, essential for accurate diagnostics in dentistry.</p></p>
