<p><strong>The Silicone Sealants for Construction Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>One Component</li><li>Two-component</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Silicone sealants in the construction market are primarily categorized into one-component and two-component types. One-component silicone sealants are ready-to-use and cure upon exposure to moisture in the air, making them convenient for various applications. In contrast, two-component silicone sealants require the mixing of a catalyst and base before application, providing enhanced durability and chemical resistance. Both types are widely used for sealing joints, gaps, and seams in buildings, ensuring long-lasting performance and protection against environmental factors.</p></p>
