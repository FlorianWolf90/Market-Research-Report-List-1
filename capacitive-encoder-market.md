<p><p>The capacitive encoder market is primarily divided into two types: linear encoders and rotary encoders. Linear encoders are designed to measure linear displacement along a straight path, often used in applications requiring precise position feedback, such as CNC machinery. Rotary encoders, on the other hand, measure rotational position or angular displacement, commonly found in motor control and automation systems. Both types utilize capacitive sensing technology to achieve high accuracy and resolution in various industrial and consumer applications.</p></p>
