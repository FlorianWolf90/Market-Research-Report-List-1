<p><p>The gynecology hysteroscopes market is divided into two main types: rigid hysteroscopes and flexible hysteroscopes. Rigid hysteroscopes feature a fixed shape and are commonly used for diagnostic procedures and surgeries due to their durability and precision. In contrast, flexible hysteroscopes offer greater maneuverability, allowing access to difficult-to-reach areas within the uterus, making them suitable for a variety of diagnostic and therapeutic applications. Both types play a crucial role in improving women's reproductive health and enabling less invasive procedures.</p></p>
