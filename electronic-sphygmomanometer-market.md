<p><p>The electronic sphygmomanometer market is primarily divided into two types: Upper Arm Electronic Sphygmomanometers and Wrist Arm Electronic Sphygmomanometers. Upper Arm models are designed to measure blood pressure at the upper arm, providing more accurate readings and are often used in clinical settings. In contrast, Wrist Arm models are compact and portable, making them suitable for home use. They are easier to use but can be less accurate than upper arm models if not positioned correctly, catering to diverse consumer needs.</p></p>
