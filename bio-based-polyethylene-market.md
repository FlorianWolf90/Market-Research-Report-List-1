<p><p>Bio-based polyethylene encompasses three key types: HDPE (High-Density Polyethylene), LLDPE (Linear Low-Density Polyethylene), and LDPE (Low-Density Polyethylene). HDPE is known for its strength and rigidity, making it suitable for packaging, containers, and pipes. LLDPE offers flexibility and impact resistance, ideal for films and bags. LDPE is characterized by its low density and is used for flexible packaging and plastic wraps. All these types are derived from renewable resources, promoting sustainability in the plastics industry.</p></p>
