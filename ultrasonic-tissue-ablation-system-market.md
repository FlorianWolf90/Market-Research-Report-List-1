<p><strong>The Ultrasonic Tissue Ablation System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>HIFU</li><li>MRgFUS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Ultrasonic Tissue Ablation System market is primarily divided into two types: High-Intensity Focused Ultrasound (HIFU) and Magnetic Resonance-guided Focused Ultrasound (MRgFUS). HIFU utilizes concentrated ultrasound waves to target and destroy tissue non-invasively, often used in oncology and cosmetic procedures. In contrast, MRgFUS combines ultrasound with magnetic resonance imaging, allowing for real-time visualization and precise targeting of tissue, enhancing safety and efficacy in treatments such as uterine fibroids and pain management. Both technologies are advancing in non-invasive therapeutic applications.</p></p>
