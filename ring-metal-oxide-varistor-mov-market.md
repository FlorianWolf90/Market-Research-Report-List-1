<p><p>Ring Metal Oxide Varistors (MOVs) are voltage-dependent resistors, functioning as surge protection devices in electrical and electronic circuits. They offer high resistance at normal operating voltages and significantly decrease resistance during voltage surges, effectively clamping excess voltage levels to prevent damage to connected components. This technology is essential for applications in power supplies, telecommunications, automotive, and consumer electronics, ensuring reliability and longevity of systems.</p><p>The Ring Metal Oxide Varistor MOV Market is experiencing notable growth driven by the rising demand for electronic devices and the increasing focus on overvoltage protection.
