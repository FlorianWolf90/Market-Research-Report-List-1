<p><p>The PMMA bone cements market is categorized into three types based on viscosity: low, medium, and high viscosity cements. Low viscosity cements are designed for easier flow and quick setting, making them ideal for less complex procedures. Medium viscosity cements offer a balance between flowability and stability, suitable for general orthopedic applications. High viscosity cements provide enhanced mechanical properties and resistance to leakage, making them preferable for more demanding procedures, such as vertebroplasty and orthopedic implant fixation.</p></p>
