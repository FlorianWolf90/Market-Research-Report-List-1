<p><p>Quantum Dot Display (QLED) technology uses quantum dots to enhance the color and brightness of displays, providing vivid images and improved energy efficiency. The QLED market can be categorized into two main types: QDEF (Quantum Dot Enhancement Film) and traditional QLED. QDEF integrates quantum dots with existing LCD technology to boost color performance, while QLED incorporates quantum dots directly into LED backlighting for higher color accuracy and contrast. Both aim to deliver superior visual experiences in televisions and monitors.</p></p>
