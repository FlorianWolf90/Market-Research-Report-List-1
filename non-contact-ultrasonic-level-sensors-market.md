<p><p>Non-Contact Ultrasonic Level Sensors are advanced devices that utilize ultrasonic waves to measure the level of liquids and solids without the need for direct contact. These sensors emit ultrasonic pulses and analyze the time it takes for the pulse to be reflected back, calculating the distance to the surface of the material being measured. <p><p>Non-contact ultrasonic level sensors are classified into two main types: switch type and continuous type. Switch type sensors provide on/off control, detecting the presence or absence of liquid at a specific level, making them ideal for applications requiring level alarms. In contrast, continuous type sensors measure the level of liquid in real-time, providing precise data for monitoring and control. Both types leverage ultrasonic waves to measure distances without physical contact, enhancing reliability and reducing maintenance requirements in various industries.</p></p>
