<p><ul><li>Chemical Pulp</li><li>Semi-Chemical Pulp</li><li>Mechanical Pulp</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The unbleached hardwood kraft pulp market encompasses three primary types: chemical pulp, semi-chemical pulp, and mechanical pulp. </p><p>Chemical pulp is produced through a chemical process that effectively removes lignin, resulting in stronger fibers suitable for high-quality paper. Semi-chemical pulp combines chemical and mechanical methods, balancing fiber strength and production efficiency, making it ideal for corrugated products. Mechanical pulp relies primarily on mechanical grinding, producing lower-quality pulp with good bulk and opacity but limited strength, often used in newsprint and other low-cost applications.</p></p>
