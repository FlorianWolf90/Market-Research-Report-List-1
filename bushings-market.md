<p><p>The bushing market is categorized primarily into three insulation types: porcelain, paper, and resin. Porcelain insulators are known for their high dielectric strength and durability, making them suitable for outdoor applications. Paper insulation offers good electrical properties and is often used in transformer bushings, but has limitations in moisture resistance. Resin insulation provides enhanced mechanical strength and resistance to environmental factors, making it ideal for compact and high-performance applications. Each type addresses specific requirements, influencing market dynamics and demand.</p></p>
