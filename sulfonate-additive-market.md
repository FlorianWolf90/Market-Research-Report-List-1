Sodium sulfonate acts as an efficient emulsifier and dispersant. Calcium sulfonate offers superior anti-wear and corrosion protection in automotive applications. Magnesium sulfonate is prized for its exceptional detergency and low-temperature performance, making it suitable for high-performance lubricants.</p></p>
