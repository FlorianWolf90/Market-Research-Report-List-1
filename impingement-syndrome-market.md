<p><ul><li>Structural Impingement</li><li>Functional Impingement</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Impingement Syndrome refers to a condition where tendons or bursa in the shoulder become irritated or inflamed due to compression. The market can be segmented into two main types: Structural Impingement, which arises from anatomical abnormalities like bone spurs or abnormal acromion shapes, leading to limited space in the shoulder joint; and Functional Impingement, caused by muscle imbalances or poor posture that affect shoulder mechanics. Each type necessitates different treatment strategies, influencing market demand for therapeutic solutions.</p></p>
