<p><p>Ambulatory Blood Pressure Monitoring (ABPM) devices are categorized mainly into Ordinary ABPM and Mobile-based ABPM. In contrast, Mobile-based ABPM integrates smartphone technology, allowing users to monitor their blood pressure through compatible apps. This type often enhances user experience with real-time data access and cloud connectivity, promoting better management of blood pressure health in a more convenient and user-friendly manner.</p></p>
