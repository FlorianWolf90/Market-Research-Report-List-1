<p><p>The light vehicle clutch market comprises several types, each serving unique functions. Wet friction clutches utilize oil for cooling and lubrication, enhancing durability and performance. Dry friction clutches operate without lubrication and are commonly used for their simplicity and cost-effectiveness. Electromagnetic clutches engage and disengage using electromagnetic forces, providing precise control in various applications. Other types may include advanced technologies like ceramic or composite clutches, each contributing to improved vehicle performance and efficiency in different driving conditions.</p></p>
