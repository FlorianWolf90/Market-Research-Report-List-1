<p><strong>The Liquid Tumor Biopsy Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ctDNA</li><li>CTC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The liquid tumor biopsy market is primarily categorized into two types: circulating tumor DNA (ctDNA) and circulating tumor cells (CTC). ctDNA involves analyzing small fragments of tumor-derived DNA found in the bloodstream, providing insights into genetic mutations and treatment response. In contrast, CTCs focus on isolating whole cells shed from solid tumors into circulation, offering information on tumor characteristics and metastatic potential. Both types are pivotal for non-invasive cancer diagnosis, personalized treatment strategies, and monitoring disease progression.</p></p>
