<p><p>The crusher market is categorized into various types, including jaw crushers, gyratory crushers, cone crushers, and others. Cone crushers offer versatility and fine particle reduction in secondary and tertiary applications. The "others" category encompasses specialized crushers, such as impact and roller crushers, catering to niche needs within the crushing industry. Each type serves distinct functions based on material properties and application requirements.</p></p>
