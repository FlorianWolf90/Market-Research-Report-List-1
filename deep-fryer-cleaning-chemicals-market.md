Foam chemicals offer a thick, clingy consistency that enhances cleaning efficiency by allowing prolonged contact with surfaces. Tablet chemicals provide a convenient, pre-measured option that dissolves in water, ensuring consistent formulation and ease of use, particularly in high-volume applications.</p></p>
