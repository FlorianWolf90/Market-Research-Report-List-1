<p><p>The aerogel felt market features two primary types: hydrophilic and hydrophobic. Hydrophilic aerogel felt attracts and absorbs moisture, making it suitable for applications in humid environments, improving thermal insulation while managing moisture. In contrast, hydrophobic aerogel felt repels water, providing excellent thermal resistance and durability, ideal for outdoor or high-moisture applications where water resistance is crucial. Both types are utilized across various industries, including construction, aerospace, and electronics, due to their lightweight and superior insulating properties.</p></p>
