Carbon brakes are lightweight and offer superior thermal performance, making them ideal for high-speed aircraft and military applications. They provide better stopping power and are more resistant to wear. In contrast, steel brakes are generally more economical and durable, suitable for a wide range of aircraft but may be heavier and less efficient under extreme conditions. Both types serve critical roles in aircraft safety and performance.</p></p>
