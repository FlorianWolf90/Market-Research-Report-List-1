<p><p>Ethylene Acrylate Rubber (AEM) is a versatile thermoplastic elastomer known for its excellent resistance to heat, oxidation, and chemical exposure. The AEM market is categorized into two types: Type I and Type II. Type I AEM typically features higher ethylene content, enhancing thermal stability and providing improved elasticity. Type II AEM, on the other hand, offers better resilience and is often used in applications demanding high flexibility and durability. Both types are essential for various industrial applications, including automotive and electrical components.</p></p>
