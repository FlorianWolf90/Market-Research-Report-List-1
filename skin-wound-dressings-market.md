<p><ul><li>Passive Dressing</li><li>Bioactive Dressing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The skin wound dressings market is primarily categorized into passive and bioactive dressings. Passive dressings serve as a protective barrier, promoting a moist environment while allowing for gas exchange, yet they do not actively participate in wound healing. In contrast, bioactive dressings contain active components, such as growth factors or antimicrobial agents, that facilitate and enhance the healing process. These dressings respond dynamically to wound conditions, aiming to accelerate recovery and minimize complications.</p></p>
