<p><p>The tissue ablation device market encompasses various technologies used for targeted tissue removal or destruction. Radiofrequency ablation utilizes electrical energy to create heat, effectively destroying tissue. Ultrasound ablation employs high-frequency sound waves to achieve similar results, while laser-based ablation uses focused light to vaporize tissue. Cryoablation involves freezing tissue to induce cell death. Other methods may include microwave or dielectric ablation techniques, offering alternatives based on specific clinical requirements and patient needs. Each type has unique applications in medical procedures.</p></p>
