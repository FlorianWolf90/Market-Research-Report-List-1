<p><p>Chemical feed systems encompass various pump types used to accurately dispense chemicals in industrial applications. Solenoid metering pumps utilize electromagnetic force for precise dosing, ideal for low-flow applications. Peristaltic metering pumps operate by compressing a flexible tube to move fluids, suitable for sanitary processes. Diaphragm metering pumps use a diaphragm to create a vacuum and displace liquid, providing excellent reliability and chemical compatibility. Other types may include screw pumps and gear pumps, each serving specific operational needs.</p></p>
