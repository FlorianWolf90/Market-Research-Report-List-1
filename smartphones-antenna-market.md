<p><p>The smartphone antenna market features several key types, including stamping antennas, FPC antennas, and LDS antennas. Stamping antennas are cost-effective, using metal sheets for structure, suitable for high-volume production. FPC antennas utilize flexible printed circuits, allowing for compact designs that fit various smartphone shapes. LDS antennas leverage laser direct structuring technology, enabling intricate patterns for enhanced performance and miniaturization. Each type caters to specific design requirements, ensuring optimal connectivity and performance in modern smartphones.</p></p>
