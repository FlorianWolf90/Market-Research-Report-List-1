<p><p>The PV silicon wafer market is primarily divided into two types: monocrystalline and polycrystalline silicon wafers. Monocrystalline silicon wafers are made from a single crystal structure, offering higher efficiency and better performance in limited spaces, making them ideal for residential and commercial applications. On the other hand, polycrystalline silicon wafers consist of multiple crystal grains, resulting in a slightly lower efficiency but at a lower cost, making them a popular choice for large-scale solar projects and budget-conscious consumers.</p></p>
