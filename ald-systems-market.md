<p><strong>The ALD Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PE-ALD</li><li>Thermal ALD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Atomic Layer Deposition (ALD) systems operate primarily in two market types: Plasma-Enhanced ALD (PE-ALD) and Thermal ALD. PE-ALD utilizes plasma to enhance the chemical reactions during film deposition, allowing for lower temperatures and improved material properties, making it suitable for advanced semiconductor applications. In contrast, Thermal ALD relies on thermal energy to facilitate reactions, offering precise control over film thickness and uniformity, predominantly used in traditional semiconductor and advanced materials manufacturing, where process conditions are critical.</p></p>
