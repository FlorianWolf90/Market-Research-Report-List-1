<p><p>Hydraulic guillotine shears are categorized into three main types: manual, semi-automatic, and fully automatic. Manual shears require operator input for each cut, offering high control but slower operation. Semi-automatic models automate some processes, reducing effort and increasing speed while still needing manual adjustments. Fully automatic shears operate with minimal human intervention, featuring advanced controls for high precision and efficiency, suitable for large-scale production. Each type serves varying operational needs and production volumes in industries like metal fabrication.</p></p>
