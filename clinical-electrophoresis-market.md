<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Clinical Electrophoresis Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Slab Electrophoresis</li><li>Zone Electrophoresis</li><li>Paper Electrophoresis</li><li>Gel Electrophoresis</li><li>Isoelectric-Focusing</li><li>Immune-Electrophoresis</li><li>Capillary Electrophoresis</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The clinical electrophoresis market includes various techniques used for separating biomolecules based on their charge and size. Slab electrophoresis and gel electrophoresis utilize a gel matrix for separation, while zone electrophoresis involves migration through a solid medium. Paper electrophoresis employs filter paper as a medium. Isoelectric focusing separates proteins based on their isoelectric points. Immune-electrophoresis combines electrophoresis with immunological techniques to identify proteins. Capillary electrophoresis uses narrow capillaries for rapid separation, offering high resolution and efficiency in analysis.</p></p>
