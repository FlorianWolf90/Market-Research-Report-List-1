<p><p>The EV High Voltage Cables market is primarily segmented into shielded and unshielded cables. Shielded cables are designed with protective layers to prevent electromagnetic interference, ensuring reliable performance and safety in electric vehicles. They are essential for high-performance applications where signal integrity is crucial. Unshielded cables, on the other hand, lack this protective layer, making them simpler and often more cost-effective, but potentially more susceptible to interference. Both types play vital roles in the overall efficiency and functionality of EV systems.</p></p>
