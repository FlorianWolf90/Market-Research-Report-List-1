<p><ul><li>Oil-free</li><li>Oil-injected</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rotary screw compressors market is primarily divided into two types: oil-free and oil-injected. Oil-free compressors use advanced technologies to eliminate oil contamination, making them ideal for applications requiring purity, such as pharmaceuticals and food processing. Conversely, oil-injected compressors utilize oil to lubricate the compression process, enhancing efficiency and cooling but potentially introducing oil into the compressed air. Each type serves specific industry needs, with oil-free options prioritizing air quality and oil-injected variants focusing on performance and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
