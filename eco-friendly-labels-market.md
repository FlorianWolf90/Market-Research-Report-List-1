<p><p>The eco-friendly labels market comprises three main types: recycled materials, recyclable materials, and renewable materials. Recyclable materials, though not recycled yet, can be processed after use, encouraging responsible disposal and resource recovery. Renewable materials are sourced from resources that can regenerate over time, such as plants, reducing reliance on finite fossil resources. Together, these categories promote sustainability and reduce environmental impact in various industries.</p></p>
