<p><strong>The Phone-based Authentication Solutions Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Factor Certification</li><li>Multifactor Certification</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Phone-based authentication solutions encompass various market types, primarily including Single Factor Certification and Multifactor Certification. Single Factor Certification typically relies on one method, such as a one-time password (OTP) sent via SMS, to verify user identity. In contrast, Multifactor Certification strengthens security by requiring two or more verification methods, such as a combination of an OTP and biometric data or a security token. This layered approach offers enhanced protection against unauthorized access, addressing the evolving challenges in digital security.</p></p>
