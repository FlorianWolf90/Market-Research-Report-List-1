<p><p>Molten Salt Thermal Energy Storage (TES) is a technology used in solar power plants that efficiently stores and delivers energy. In tower-type solar power systems, concentrated sunlight is focused onto a receiver atop a tower, heating molten salt for energy storage. Dish concentrating solar power systems utilize parabolic dishes to concentrate sunlight onto a receiver that heats a working fluid. Both technologies enable prolonged energy generation, harnessing solar power for continuous and reliable electricity supply, enhancing grid stability.</p></p>
