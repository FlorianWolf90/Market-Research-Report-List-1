<p><p>The Power Equipment Testing Market encompasses two main segments: Power Primary Equipment Testing and Power Secondary Equipment Testing. Power Primary Equipment Testing focuses on assessing high-voltage equipment like transformers, circuit breakers, and transmission lines to ensure they operate safely and efficiently. In contrast, Power Secondary Equipment Testing involves evaluating systems associated with power distribution, such as protection relays, capacitor banks, and control panels. Together, these testing types ensure reliability, safety, and performance across the electrical infrastructure.</p></p>
