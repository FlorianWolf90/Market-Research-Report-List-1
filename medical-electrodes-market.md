<p><p>The medical electrodes market comprises two main types: disposable and reusable electrodes. Disposable electrodes are designed for single-use, offering enhanced hygiene and convenience, making them ideal for short-term applications in diagnostics and monitoring. They are often used in settings like hospitals and clinics. In contrast, reusable electrodes are designed for multiple uses, providing cost-effectiveness over time and reducing medical waste. They are typically employed in long-term procedures and require careful cleaning and maintenance between uses to ensure safety and efficacy.</p></p>
