<p><p>The EVC charging station market includes two main types: on-board and off-board chargers. On-board chargers are integrated into the electric vehicle (EV), converting AC power to DC for battery charging. They are compact and convenient for home or slow charging. Off-board chargers are external units, typically used in public or fast-charging stations, delivering high-power DC directly to the EV battery. Both types cater to different charging needs, balancing convenience, speed, and infrastructure requirements in the evolving EV ecosystem.</p></p>
