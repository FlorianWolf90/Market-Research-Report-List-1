<p><p>The airflow meters market is primarily divided into two main types: analog and digital airflow meters. Analog type airflow meters utilize mechanical components, providing a visual representation of airflow measurements through gauges and dials. They are simpler and often more affordable. Conversely, digital type airflow meters employ electronic sensors to offer precise, real-time measurements and enhanced features such as data storage and connectivity options. Digital meters are increasingly favored for their accuracy and ease of integration in modern monitoring systems.</p></p>
