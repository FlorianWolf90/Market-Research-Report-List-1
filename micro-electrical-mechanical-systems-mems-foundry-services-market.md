<p><p>The Micro-Electrical Mechanical Systems (MEMS) foundry services market is categorized into two main types: pure-play and integrated device manufacturer (IDM) markets. Pure-play foundries focus exclusively on manufacturing MEMS devices for various clients, providing specialized fabrication services without designing their own components. In contrast, IDM companies combine design, manufacturing, and packaging capabilities, producing their MEMS products in-house. This differentiation allows customers to choose between dedicated specialized services or a more integrated approach based on their needs.</p></p>
