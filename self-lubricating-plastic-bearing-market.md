<p><strong>The Self-lubricating Plastic Bearing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Plastic Rolling Bearings</li><li>Plastic Sliding Bearings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The self-lubricating plastic bearing market is segmented into two main types: plastic rolling bearings and plastic sliding bearings. Plastic rolling bearings utilize balls or rollers to reduce friction, providing smooth motion and improved load capacity, making them ideal for high-speed applications. On the other hand, plastic sliding bearings rely on low-friction surfaces that allow for smooth sliding motion without rolling elements, offering simplicity and reliability in applications with limited space. Both types benefit from reduced maintenance and corrosion resistance.</p></p>
