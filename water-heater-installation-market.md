<p><p>The water heater installation market comprises two primary types: tank storage and tankless systems. Tank storage water heaters maintain a reservoir of hot water, providing a ready supply but consuming energy to keep the water hot. Conversely, tankless water heaters heat water on demand, offering an efficient option with no standby heat loss, although installation can be more complex. Both systems cater to varying consumer needs, preferences, and energy usage, influencing their market growth and adoption rates.</p></p>
