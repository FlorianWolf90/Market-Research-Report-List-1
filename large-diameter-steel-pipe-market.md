<p><p>The Large Diameter Steel Pipe market encompasses several types, including LSAW (Longitudinal Submerged Arc Welded) and SSAW (Spiral Submerged Arc Welded) pipes. LSAW pipes are produced using a straight seam welding process, offering high strength and precision, making them suitable for high-pressure applications. Conversely, SSAW pipes are created by spirally welding flat steel strips, which allows for more flexibility in size and shape, ideal for various construction and infrastructure projects. Other types may include seamless and coated pipes, catering to specific industry needs.</p></p>
