<p><p>Dry heat sterilizers are devices that utilize high temperatures to eliminate all forms of microbial life, including bacteria, viruses, and spores. Unlike steam sterilization, which uses moisture to kill microorganisms, dry heat sterilization relies on hot air that circulates within the chamber, typically operating at temperatures between 160°C to 180°C. This method is particularly suitable for sterilizing materials that may be damaged by moisture, such as certain surgical instruments and powders.</p><p>The Dry Heat Sterilizers Market is experiencing robust growth, driven by the increasing demand for effective sterilization methods in healthcare settings, the rising prevalence of healthcare-associated infections, and the growing pharmaceutical and biotechnology sectors.
