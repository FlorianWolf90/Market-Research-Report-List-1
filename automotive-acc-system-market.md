<p><ul><li>Radar</li><li>LIDAR</li><li>Sensor Fusion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive Adaptive Cruise Control (ACC) system market comprises three main types: radar, LIDAR, and sensor fusion. Radar systems utilize radio waves to detect obstacles and maintain safe distances, offering reliability in various weather conditions. LIDAR, using laser beams, provides high-resolution 3D maps of surroundings for precise obstacle detection. Sensor fusion combines data from multiple sensors, integrating information from radar, LIDAR, and cameras to enhance accuracy and robustness. Together, these technologies enhance vehicle safety and automation in advanced driver-assistance systems.</p></p>
