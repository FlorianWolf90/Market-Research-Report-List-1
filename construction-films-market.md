<p><strong>The Construction Films Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>LLDPE</li><li>LDPE</li><li>HDPE</li><li>PP/BOPP</li><li>PET/BOPET</li><li>POLYAMIDE/BOPA</li><li>PVB</li><li>PVC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The construction films market comprises various polymer types, each serving distinct applications. LLDPE (Linear Low-Density Polyethylene) offers flexibility and strength, ideal for moisture barriers. LDPE (Low-Density Polyethylene) is lightweight and cost-effective, suited for general protective purposes. HDPE (High-Density Polyethylene) provides durability, often used in heavy-duty applications. PP/BOPP (Polypropylene/Biaxially Oriented Polypropylene) is known for its clarity and resistance to chemicals. PET/BOPET (Polyethylene Terephthalate) is valued for its strength and thermal stability. Polyamide (Nylon) offers toughness, while PVB (Polyvinyl Butyral) is favored for adhesion and security. PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride) is versatile for various structural applications.</p></p>
