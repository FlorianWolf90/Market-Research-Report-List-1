<p><p>OLAP systems are categorized into several market types based on their data storage and processing methods. ROLAP (Relational OLAP) utilizes relational databases for data storage, enabling complex queries, while MOLAP (Multidimensional OLAP) stores data in multidimensional arrays, offering faster query performance. HMOLAP (Hybrid MOLAP) combines both strategies, leveraging the strengths of each. Other market types may include DOLAP (Desktop OLAP) and WOLAP (Web OLAP), which focus on specific deployment environments and user accessibility, catering to diverse analytical needs.</p></p>
