<p><ul><li>Screw</li><li>Scroll</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial rotary air compressor market is primarily segmented into three types: screw compressors, scroll compressors, and others. Screw compressors utilize twin helical screws for high efficiency and continuous operation, making them ideal for large-scale applications. Scroll compressors employ spiral designs for generating high pressure with minimal vibration and noise, suitable for small to medium applications. The "others" category includes various rotary designs like vane compressors, each serving niche applications based on specific operational needs and efficiency requirements.</p></p>
