<p><p>The ATP Assay Kit market includes three main types: Luminometric, Colorimetric, and Fluorimetric. Luminometric ATP Assay Kits utilize bioluminescence to quantify ATP levels, providing high sensitivity and rapid results. Colorimetric Kits employ color change reactions to detect ATP, making them simpler but generally less sensitive. Fluorimetric Kits utilize fluorescence intensity to measure ATP concentration, offering good sensitivity and specificity. Each type serves different applications in research, clinical diagnostics, and environmental monitoring, catering to varied user needs.</p></p>
