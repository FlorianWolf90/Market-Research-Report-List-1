<p><p>The acidic paint remover market is categorized primarily into oily and paste types. In contrast, paste-type removers have a thicker consistency, allowing them to adhere to the surface longer, effectively breaking down stubborn coatings without dripping. Both types are effective for various applications, catering to different user preferences and specific project requirements in paint removal.</p></p>
