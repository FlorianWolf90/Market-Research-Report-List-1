<p><p>The engine oil pressure sensor market is primarily categorized into three types: Ceramic Type, Film Type, and Others. Ceramic type sensors utilize piezoelectric materials for precise pressure readings, offering durability and resistance to high temperatures. Film type sensors rely on thin films that change resistance with pressure variations, providing accurate measurements in a compact design. The "Others" category includes various innovative sensors, such as hybrid or digital types, catering to diverse automotive applications and enhancing engine monitoring capabilities.</p></p>
