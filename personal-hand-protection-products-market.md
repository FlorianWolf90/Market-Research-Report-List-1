<p><p>The personal hand protection products market is primarily divided into nitrile gloves and PVC gloves. Nitrile gloves, made from synthetic rubber, offer superior chemical resistance, durability, and puncture protection, making them ideal for medical, industrial, and laboratory applications. In contrast, PVC gloves, crafted from polyvinyl chloride, provide a cost-effective solution for applications with minimal risk of exposure, commonly used in food handling and light-duty tasks. Both types cater to various safety needs across different sectors, enhancing worker protection.</p></p>
