<p><p>The pedal sensors market encompasses various technologies that detect the position and movement of pedals in vehicles. Resistive sensors use a variable resistor to measure pedal position, offering simplicity and reliability. Magnetic sensors utilize magnetic fields to detect pedal angle and movement, providing durability and resistance to environmental factors. Inductive sensors operate by measuring changes in inductance caused by pedal movement, known for their precision and robustness. Each type caters to different automotive applications, enhancing performance and safety.</p></p>
