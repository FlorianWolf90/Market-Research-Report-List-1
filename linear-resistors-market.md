<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Linear Resistors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>VDR Resistor</li><li>PTC Resistor</li><li>NTC Resistor</li><li>LDR Resistor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The linear resistors market includes various types; VDR (Voltage Dependent Resistor) resistors protect circuits by changing resistance with voltage fluctuations. PTC (Positive Temperature Coefficient) resistors increase resistance with temperature, often used in overcurrent protection. NTC (Negative Temperature Coefficient) resistors decrease resistance as temperature rises, commonly utilized in temperature sensing applications. LDR (Light Dependent Resistor) resistors change resistance based on light exposure, making them ideal for light-sensing applications. Each type serves distinct roles in electronic systems, influencing market demand and applications.</p></p>
