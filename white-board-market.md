<p><p>The whiteboard market features three primary types: melamine, porcelain, and glass whiteboards. Melamine whiteboards are cost-effective, suited for light use, but may wear over time. Glass whiteboards offer a modern aesthetic, with a sleek surface that resists ghosting and marks, while being easy to clean.
