<p><p>Machine Direction Orientation (MDO) systems enhance the mechanical properties of films by aligning polymer chains in the machine direction. The Horizontal Type MDO systems operate by stretching film horizontally, facilitating uniform orientation and improved clarity. Vertical Type MDO systems stretch films vertically, often resulting in increased strength and barrier properties. Other market types can include specialized or hybrid systems that integrate both horizontal and vertical orientations, offering customized solutions for specific applications in packaging and manufacturing.</p></p>
