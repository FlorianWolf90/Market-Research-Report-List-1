<p><ul><li>Wet Grinding</li><li>Dry Grinding</li><li>Fermentation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Household Food Waste Disposer market comprises three primary types: wet grinding, dry grinding, and fermentation. Wet grinding units use water to break down food waste into a slurry, facilitating easy disposal. Dry grinding systems operate without water, shredding waste into smaller pieces for simpler handling. Fermentation units convert food waste into compost or other useful materials through natural decomposition processes, promoting sustainable waste management. Each type offers unique benefits catering to various consumer needs and environmental concerns.</p></p>
