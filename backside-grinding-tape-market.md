<p><p>Backside grinding tape is essential in semiconductor manufacturing, used to support wafers during the grinding process. The market can be segmented into UV type and non-UV type. UV type tape is designed to be cured using ultraviolet light, providing strong adhesion while allowing for easy removal post-processing. Non-UV type tape, on the other hand, relies on adhesive properties without requiring UV curing, making it suitable for applications where exposure to light is limited. Both types cater to specific manufacturing needs in the industry.</p></p>
