<p><ul><li>Casting Molding</li><li>Press Molding</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The kitchen quartz countertops market features various manufacturing types, primarily casting molding and press molding. Casting molding involves pouring a mixture of crushed quartz, resin, and pigments into a mold, allowing for intricate designs and textures. This method typically results in a more uniform surface. In contrast, press molding combines heat and pressure to form quartz slabs, offering enhanced durability and faster production. Both methods produce high-quality countertops, catering to different aesthetic and functional preferences in kitchen design.</p></p>
