<p><p>The wristwatch market is broadly categorized into mechanical and electronic watches. They are typically higher-priced and valued for their artistry. Electronic watches, on the other hand, use battery-powered quartz movements or digital displays, offering precision, affordability, and modern functionality like fitness tracking.
