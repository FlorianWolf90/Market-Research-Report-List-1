<p><strong>The RF Probes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive Type</li><li>Active Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The RF probes market is divided into two main types: passive and active. Passive RF probes rely on external power sources and do not amplify the signal; they are typically used for basic measurements and diagnostics. In contrast, active RF probes have built-in amplification, allowing them to measure weaker signals with greater accuracy and speed. Both types serve essential roles in various applications, including telecommunications, automotive testing, and electronics debugging, catering to different user needs in signal testing and analysis.</p></p>
