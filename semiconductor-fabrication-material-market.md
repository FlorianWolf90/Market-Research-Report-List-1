<p><p>The semiconductor fabrication material market comprises various essential components. Silicon wafers serve as the foundational substrates for circuits. CMP slurry and pads are crucial for chemical-mechanical planarization. Gases are employed in various deposition processes. Sputter targets are materials used in physical vapor deposition. Photoresist ancillaries support photoresist applications, while "other" includes miscellaneous materials.</p></p>
