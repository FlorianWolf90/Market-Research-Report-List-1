<p><ul><li>C Type</li><li>B Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Acousto-Optic Q-Switch market is categorized into two main types: C-Type and B-Type. C-Type Q-Switches utilize a collinear arrangement where the acoustic wave and light beam travel in the same direction, leading to efficient modulation and high power handling. B-Type Q-Switches, on the other hand, operate in a non-collinear configuration, allowing for better control over beam quality and temporal resolution. Each type serves specific applications in laser technology, impacting performance and operational efficiency.</p></p>
