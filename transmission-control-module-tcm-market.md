<p><p>The Transmission Control Module (TCM) market encompasses various types, notably the Double Clutch Gearbox Control Unit and the Electro-Hydraulic Gearbox Control Module. In contrast, the Electro-Hydraulic Gearbox Control Module integrates hydraulic systems with electronic controls for smooth gear transitions and improved responsiveness. Both types cater to the growing demand for advanced transmission systems in modern vehicles, contributing to enhanced driving experiences.</p></p>
