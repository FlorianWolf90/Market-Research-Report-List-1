<p><strong>The Anti-lock Brake Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>One-channel ABS</li><li>Two-channel ABS</li><li>Three-channel ABS</li><li>Four-channel ABS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Anti-lock Brake Systems (ABS) are categorized based on the number of channels controlling wheel braking. One-channel ABS manages braking for a single wheel, primarily utilized in older or simpler vehicles. Two-channel ABS regulates front wheels separately, enhancing stability and control. Three-channel ABS offers more advanced control with separate management for two front and one rear wheel, suitable for larger vehicles. Four-channel ABS provides full control over each wheel, optimizing braking performance and safety in modern vehicles.</p></p>
