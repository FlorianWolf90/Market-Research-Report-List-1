<p><p>The General Aviation Engines market comprises four main types: </p><p>1. **Turboprop Engines**: Ideal for regional flights, utilizing a gas turbine to drive a propeller, offering efficiency at lower speeds.  </p><p>   </p><p>2. **Turbofan Engines**: Primarily used in business jets and larger aircraft, providing high efficiency and thrust, featuring a fan to generate additional airflow.  </p><p>3. **Turboshaft Engines**: Commonly found in helicopters, powering the rotor systems and delivering high power-to-weight ratios.  </p><p>4. **Piston Engines**: Often used in light aircraft, relying on reciprocating pistons for propulsion, favored for their simplicity and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
