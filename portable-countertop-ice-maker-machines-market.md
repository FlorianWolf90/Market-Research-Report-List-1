<p><ul><li>With Side Tank</li><li>Without Side Tank</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Portable countertop ice maker machines are categorized into two main types based on their water supply mechanisms: those with a side tank and those without. Machines with a side tank have an additional reservoir, allowing for more extended, uninterrupted ice production, making them ideal for gatherings. In contrast, machines without a side tank rely on direct water input or their built-in reservoir, offering a more compact design but requiring more frequent refilling. Both types cater to different consumer needs based on convenience and capacity.</p></p>
