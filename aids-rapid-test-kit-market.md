<p><p>The AIDS Rapid Test Kit market includes two primary types: blood tests and saliva tests. Blood tests typically involve pricking a fingertip to obtain a small blood sample, allowing for quick detection of HIV antibodies. Saliva tests, on the other hand, use oral fluid to determine HIV status, providing a less invasive option. Both methods offer rapid results, enhancing access to testing and facilitating early diagnosis and treatment, ultimately contributing to improved public health outcomes in the fight against HIV/AIDS.</p></p>
