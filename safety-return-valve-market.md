<p><p>The Safety Return Valve market primarily includes two types: Spring Type Safety Return Valves and Rod Safety Return Valves. Spring Type Safety Return Valves utilize a spring mechanism that helps maintain pressure control and ensures safe discharge during overload conditions. In contrast, Rod Safety Return Valves employ a rod system to manage flow and provide precise regulation. Both types are crucial in various industries, including oil and gas, manufacturing, and water treatment, to prevent system failures and ensure operational safety.</p></p>
