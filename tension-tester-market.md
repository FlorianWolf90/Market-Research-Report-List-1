<p><p>The tension tester market is divided into two main types: digital and analog. Digital tension testers utilize electronic sensors and displays, providing precise measurements and often advanced features like data logging and connectivity options. They are favored for their accuracy and ease of use. Analog tension testers, on the other hand, employ mechanical gauges to measure tension, offering a simpler, more straightforward approach. While they may lack some advanced functionalities, they can be more durable and cost-effective for basic applications.</p></p>
