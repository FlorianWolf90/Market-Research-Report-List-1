<p><p>The medical shower trolleys market consists of three primary types: manual, electric, and hydraulic shower trolleys. Manual shower trolleys rely on human effort for movement and adjustments, making them cost-effective and simple to operate. Electric shower trolleys offer powered functions for height adjustments and mobility, enhancing ease of use for caregivers and improving patient comfort. Hydraulic shower trolleys utilize hydraulic systems for smooth height and position adjustments, providing stability and support, particularly for heavier patients or those with mobility challenges.</p></p>
