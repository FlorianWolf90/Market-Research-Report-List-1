<p><p>The singlet lenses market includes various types designed for specific optical applications. Plano-convex lenses focus light and are commonly used in projectors, while plano-concave lenses diverge light and are often employed in beam expanders. Biconvex lenses also converge light for applications like magnifiers, whereas double concave lenses disperse light, ideal for laser systems. Other lens types cater to specialized needs, including aspheric and gradient-index lenses, enhancing performance in optical systems across industries such as imaging, telecommunications, and automation.</p></p>
