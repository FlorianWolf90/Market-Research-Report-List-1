<p><p>Bottle filling and sealing machines are categorized into fully-automatic and semi-automatic types. Fully-automatic machines handle the entire process, from filling to sealing, with minimal human intervention, ensuring high efficiency and consistent output. They are ideal for large-scale production. In contrast, semi-automatic machines require some manual input, such as positioning bottles, while automating the filling and sealing processes. These are suitable for smaller operations or businesses looking for flexibility and lower investment costs while still achieving reasonable output.</p></p>
