<p><p>The e-bike helmet market is categorized into two main types: full face helmets and open face helmets. Full face helmets offer maximum protection by covering the entire head, making them ideal for high-speed rides or challenging terrains. They usually feature additional safety elements like a chin guard. Open face helmets, in contrast, provide a more relaxed riding experience with improved visibility and ventilation, making them popular for casual commuting and leisurely rides. Each type serves distinct preferences and safety needs.</p></p>
