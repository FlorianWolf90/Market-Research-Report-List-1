<p><ul><li>PAN Based Carbon Fiber</li><li>Pitch Based Carbon Fiber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Carbon fiber tows are categorized primarily into PAN-based and pitch-based types. PAN-based carbon fibers are derived from polyacrylonitrile, offering high tensile strength and excellent mechanical properties, making them suitable for aerospace and automotive applications. In contrast, pitch-based carbon fibers are produced from petroleum-derived pitches and are known for their high thermal conductivity and modulus, often used in applications requiring thermal resistance and electrical conductivity, such as in industrial and electronic components. Each type serves specific end-use industries based on performance characteristics.</p></p>
