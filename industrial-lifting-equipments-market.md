<p><p>The industrial lifting equipment market includes hydraulic, pneumatic, electrical, and magnetic lifting systems. Hydraulic lifting equipment uses fluid pressure to generate force, ideal for heavy loads and precise control. Pneumatic lifting equipment relies on compressed air, offering lightweight and clean operation for medium-duty tasks. Electrical lifting equipment employs motors and power sources, providing efficient and versatile solutions for various industries. Magnetic lifting equipment uses electromagnetic force to handle ferrous materials, ensuring safe and contactless lifting in metalworking and manufacturing applications. Each type caters to specific industrial needs.</p></p>
