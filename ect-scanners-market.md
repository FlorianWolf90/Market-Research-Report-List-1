<p><p>The ECT (Emission Computed Tomography) scanners market includes various technologies such as SPECT (Single Photon Emission Computed Tomography), PET (Positron Emission Tomography), and D-SPECT (Dynamic SPECT). SPECT utilizes gamma rays to assess blood flow and metabolic activity, while PET employs positron-emitting radionuclides to provide detailed imaging of physiological functions. D-SPECT enhances SPECT capabilities through dynamic imaging, enabling real-time observation. Each type serves unique diagnostic purposes, catering to diverse medical needs in oncology, cardiology, and neurology.</p></p>
