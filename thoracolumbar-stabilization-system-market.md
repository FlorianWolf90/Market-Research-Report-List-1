<p><p>The thoracolumbar stabilization system market comprises two primary types: posterior stabilization systems and anterolateral stabilization systems. Posterior stabilization systems focus on providing support from the back, utilizing devices like rods and screws to stabilize the spine following injury or surgery. Anterolateral stabilization systems, on the other hand, stabilize the spine from the front and side, often implementing plates and screws to enhance structural support. Both systems aim to promote healing and restore function in thoracolumbar injuries.</p></p>
