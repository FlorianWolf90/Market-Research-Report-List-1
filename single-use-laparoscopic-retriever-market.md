<p><strong>The Single Use Laparoscopic Retriever Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Detachable</li><li>Non-detachable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The single-use laparoscopic retriever market offers two primary types: detachable and non-detachable devices. Detachable retrievers allow surgeons to remove a captured specimen without withdrawing the entire instrument, enhancing efficiency during procedures. On the other hand, non-detachable retrievers are designed for one-time use, where the entire instrument, including the captured specimen, is removed together. Both types cater to different surgical needs, with detachable models often preferred for their convenience and flexibility, while non-detachable models emphasize straightforward usage and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
