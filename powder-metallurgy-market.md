<p><p>Powder Metallurgy (PM) is a manufacturing process that involves the production of metal parts by compacting powdered materials and sintering them at elevated temperatures. The Powder Metallurgy Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.10% during the forecast period, driven by increasing demand for lightweight and durable materials, along with innovations in production processes.</p><p>Market growth is further supported by the rising adoption of PM for producing intricate shapes that would be challenging to achieve through conventional methods. Additionally, advancements in additive manufacturing technologies are facilitating new applications of PM, expanding its usage in high-performance components. Environmental concerns also play a role, as PM processes generally produce less waste compared to traditional metallurgy. The industrial shift towards automation and digitization, as well as the growing trend of recycling metal powders, are influencing market dynamics. Overall, the Powder Metallurgy Market is poised for sustained expansion, owing to its versatility and efficiency in meeting the evolving demands of modern manufacturing.</p></p>
