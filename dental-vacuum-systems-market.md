<p><p>Dental vacuum systems are categorized into two main types: Type I and Type II. Type I systems are central vacuum systems, typically installed in larger dental practices or clinics, providing consistent suction across multiple operatories. These systems usually require extensive plumbing and mechanical setup. Type II systems are decentralised, often portable or self-contained units used in smaller practices or specific procedures, offering flexibility and easier installation. Both types serve critical roles in maintaining a clean and efficient dental environment.</p></p>
