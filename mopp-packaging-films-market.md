<p><p>MOPP (Mono-oriented polypropylene) packaging films are categorized by thickness into three market segments: below 50 μm, 51-100 μm, and above 100 μm. Films under 50 μm are typically used for lightweight applications, providing excellent clarity and flexibility. The 51-100 μm segment offers a balance of strength and flexibility, suitable for various packaging needs. Films above 100 μm are thicker, offering enhanced durability and protection, ideal for heavy-duty packaging applications that require greater resistance to punctures and tears.</p></p>
