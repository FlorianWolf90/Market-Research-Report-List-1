<p><p>The mobile coupon product market comprises three main types: SMS Transivers, Readable Codes or Tags, and NFC Devices. SMS Transivers allow businesses to send discounts via text messages, engaging consumers directly on their mobile devices. Readable Codes or Tags, including QR codes, enable users to scan for offers using their smartphones, providing instant access to promotions. NFC Devices allow for contactless interactions, enabling users to redeem coupons by simply tapping their smartphone near the retailer's NFC-enabled terminal. Each type enhances consumer engagement and convenience.</p></p>
