<p><p>The portable fire extinguishers market is categorized into several types based on the extinguishing agents used. Dry chemical extinguishers utilize a powder that interrupts the chemical reaction of fire, making them versatile for various fire classes. Foam extinguishers smother flames by forming a blanket, effective for liquid fires. Carbon dioxide extinguishers displace oxygen, ideal for electrical and flammable liquid fires. Water extinguishers cool down flames and are suitable for wood and paper fires. Other types include specialized extinguishers for unique fire hazards.</p></p>
