<p><ul><li>Single Electrode Voltage Clamp</li><li>Two Electrode Voltage Clamp</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The voltage clamp modules market consists of two primary types: single electrode voltage clamps and two electrode voltage clamps. Single electrode voltage clamps utilize a single electrode to measure and control the membrane potential of a cell, allowing for precise assessment of ionic currents. In contrast, two electrode voltage clamps employ two electrodes to simultaneously control and measure the voltage across a cell membrane, enabling detailed analysis of cellular excitability and ion channel function. Both types are essential in electrophysiological research and applications.</p></p>
