<p><p>The millimeter wave antenna market features two key types: Antenna in Package (AiP) and Antenna on Package (AoP). AiP integrates antennas directly into the package, optimizing size and performance for applications like smartphones and IoT devices. In contrast, AoP involves mounting antennas on the package’s surface, offering flexibility in design and improving heat dissipation while maintaining functionality. Both types cater to the growing demand for compact, high-frequency communication in various sectors, including telecommunications and automotive.</p></p>
