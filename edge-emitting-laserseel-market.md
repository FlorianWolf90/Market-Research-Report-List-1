<p><strong>The Edge Emitting Lasers(EEL) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Distributed Feedback Laser</li><li>Distributed Bragg Reflector Laser</li><li>Fabry-perot Laser</li><li>Broad Area Laser Diodes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Edge Emitting Lasers (EEL) are semiconductor lasers that emit light from their edge rather than the surface. The market comprises various types: </p><p>1. Distributed Feedback Lasers (DFB) offer stable wavelength and narrow linewidth through a built-in grating. </p><p>2. Distributed Bragg Reflector Lasers (DBR) use reflective structures for mode selection and wavelength tuning. </p><p>3. Fabry-Perot Lasers consist of two mirrors forming a resonant cavity, providing broader wavelength emission. </p><p>4. Broad Area Laser Diodes are designed for high power and wide beam output, ideal for applications needing increased illumination.</p></p>
