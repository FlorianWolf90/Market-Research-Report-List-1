<p><p>The agricultural tires market primarily features two types: bias ply and radial ply tires. Bias ply tires have crisscrossed layers of fabric under the tread, providing flexibility and durability, making them suitable for field operations. Conversely, radial ply tires utilize a radial construction that offers improved traction, reduced rolling resistance, and better fuel efficiency on various terrains. Radial tires are preferred for their enhanced performance and longevity, while bias ply tires remain reliable for specific agricultural applications.</p></p>
