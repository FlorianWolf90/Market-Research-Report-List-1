<p><p>The motorcycle immobilizer market is primarily categorized into two types: installation type and non-installation type. Installation type immobilizers are directly integrated into the motorcycle’s system, requiring professional installation to ensure effective security, typically offering higher protection levels. Non-installation type immobilizers, on the other hand, are standalone devices that can be easily attached or detached without professional help, providing convenience and flexibility. Both types aim to prevent unauthorized use, enhancing overall motorcycle security and reducing theft risk.</p></p>
