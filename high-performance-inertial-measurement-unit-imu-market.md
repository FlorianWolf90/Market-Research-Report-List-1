<p><p>The high-performance Inertial Measurement Unit (IMU) market comprises two primary types: gyroscopes and accelerometers. Gyroscopes measure angular velocity, enabling precise orientation and rotational motion tracking, essential in applications like aerospace and robotics. Accelerometers, on the other hand, gauge linear acceleration, providing data on speed and movement direction. Together, these components facilitate advanced motion sensing and navigation capabilities, driving innovations in automotive, drone, and wearable technology sectors. This synergy enhances overall system performance and reliability across various industries.</p></p>
