<p><p>The protein purification resin market is categorized into three main types: Protein A purification resin, Protein G purification resin, and Protein L purification resin. Protein A resin is primarily used for monoclonal antibody purification due to its high affinity for the Fc region. Protein G resin offers broader binding capabilities for various immunoglobulin classes. Protein L resin binds to the light chains of antibodies, making it suitable for certain applications. Each resin type serves unique needs in biopharmaceutical production and research applications.</p></p>
