Commonly used in vehicles such as trucks, buses, and heavy-duty equipment, it includes systems like engine brakes, exhaust brakes, and retarders. These systems enhance safety, reduce wear on primary brakes, and improve vehicle control, especially in challenging conditions like steep descents or heavy loads.</p><p>The Secondary Braking System Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.2% during the forecast period. <p><strong>The Secondary Braking System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hydraulic Retarder</li><li>Electromagnetic Retarder</li><li>Engine Brake</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The secondary braking system market includes various types designed to enhance vehicle safety and control. **Hydraulic retarders** use fluid resistance to slow down vehicles, offering smooth deceleration, ideal for heavy-duty applications. **Electromagnetic retarders** generate braking force through electromagnetic induction, providing efficient, wear-free braking, commonly used in buses and trucks. **Engine brakes** (Jake brakes) utilize engine compression to slow vehicles, effective in large trucks. **Others** include exhaust brakes and regenerative braking systems, each offering unique advantages for specific vehicle types and operational needs.</p></p>
