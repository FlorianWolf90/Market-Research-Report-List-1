<p><p>The Diesel EGR (Exhaust Gas Recirculation) valve market is categorized into low-pressure and high-pressure segments. Low-pressure EGR systems recirculate exhaust gases back to the engine intake at lower pressure, enhancing fuel efficiency and reducing NOx emissions in light-duty vehicles. In contrast, high-pressure EGR systems operate at higher pressure and are typically found in heavy-duty vehicles, adept at managing higher exhaust flow and delivering improved emission control. These distinct types cater to varying engine designs and regulatory requirements.</p></p>
