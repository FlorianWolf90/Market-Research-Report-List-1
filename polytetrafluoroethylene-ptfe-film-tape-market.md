<p><p>Polytetrafluoroethylene (PTFE) film tape is available in various densities catering to different applications. Low-density PTFE tape offers flexibility and is ideal for sealing low-pressure systems. Standard density balances flexibility and durability, suitable for general-purpose sealing. Medium-density tape provides enhanced mechanical strength for more demanding applications, while high-density PTFE tape is the most robust, ensuring superior performance in high-pressure environments and extreme temperatures. Each density type is designed to meet specific requirements, enhancing versatility in usage across industries.</p></p>
