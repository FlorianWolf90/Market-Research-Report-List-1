<p><p>The double-sided tape market is primarily categorized into thick and thin tape segments. Thick double-sided tapes, typically ranging from 0.5 mm to several millimeters in thickness, are often used for heavy-duty applications, providing high adhesion and structural integrity, making them ideal for bonding materials like metal and wood. In contrast, thin double-sided tapes, usually less than 0.5 mm thick, are suitable for delicate applications such as mounting pictures or electronic components, offering discreet adhesion without adding bulk to assemblies.</p></p>
