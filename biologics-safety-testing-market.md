<p><p>The Biologics Safety Testing Market encompasses various essential testing types to ensure the safety and quality of biologics. Endotoxin tests assess bacterial contamination, while sterility tests confirm the absence of viable microorganisms. Cell line authentication and characterization tests verify the identity and origin of cell lines. Bioburden tests measure the microbial load, while residual host contaminant detection tests identify any remaining host impurities. Adventitious agent detection tests screen for unexpected contaminants, and "others" includes diverse assays for comprehensive safety evaluation.</p></p>
