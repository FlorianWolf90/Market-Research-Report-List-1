<p><p>Non-Invasive Prenatal Testing (NIPT) is a genetic testing method used during pregnancy to assess the risk of certain chromosomal conditions in the fetus, such as Down syndrome and other aneuploidies. The test analyzes small fragments of fetal DNA present in a pregnant woman's blood, providing an accurate and low-risk alternative to invasive procedures like amniocentesis.</p><p>The NIPT market has seen significant growth in recent years, driven by rising awareness among expectant parents about prenatal screening and an increase in cases of advanced maternal age, which is associated with higher risks of genetic abnormalities. Technological advancements and the introduction of new, enhanced testing solutions have further fueled market expansion. Key trends include the growing acceptance of NIPT as a first-line screening method and an increase in partnerships between biotech companies and healthcare providers to enhance testing accessibility.</p><p>The Non-Invasive Prenatal Testing (NIPT) Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.8% during the forecast period, indicating a robust demand for these testing solutions as more healthcare providers adopt them into standard prenatal care protocols, improving outcomes for both mothers and babies.</p></p>

<p><p>Non-Invasive Prenatal Testing (NIPT) employs advanced technologies to assess fetal health without risking the mother or baby. It encompasses various market types: Ultrasound uses sound waves to visualize the fetus; Next-Generation Sequencing (NGS) analyzes DNA fragments for genetic conditions; Polymerase Chain Reaction (PCR) amplifies specific genetic material to detect anomalies; and Microarray examines multiple genetic markers simultaneously. These methods enhance prenatal diagnostics by providing accurate, timely information on fetal conditions, improving maternal care and decision-making.</p></p>
