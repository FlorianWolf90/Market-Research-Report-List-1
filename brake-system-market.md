<p><p>The brake system market comprises several types based on the mechanism used for braking. Mechanical brakes rely on physical force applied through levers and cables. Hydraulic brakes utilize fluid pressure to transmit force, offering enhanced stopping power. Pneumatic brakes operate using compressed air for quick and efficient braking. Electromagnetic brakes leverage electromagnetic forces for rapid stopping in electric vehicles. Combined brake systems integrate multiple technologies for improved performance and versatility, catering to a wide range of applications in automotive and industrial sectors.</p></p>
