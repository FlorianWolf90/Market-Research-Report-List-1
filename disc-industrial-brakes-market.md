<p><p>The disc industrial brakes market primarily features two types: fixed disc type and floating plate type. Fixed disc brakes are securely mounted, providing stable performance and effective braking in heavy-duty applications. Both types are crucial for various industries, offering reliable stopping power and durability under demanding conditions, thereby ensuring safety and operational efficiency in industrial machinery.</p></p>
