<p><ul><li>Thermoplastic Membrane</li><li>Synthetic Rubber (Thermoset)</li><li>Modified Bitumen</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The roofing membranes market comprises three main types: thermoplastic membranes, synthetic rubber (thermoset) membranes, and modified bitumen. Thermoplastic membranes are versatile and heat-welded for seamless joints, offering durability and weather resistance. Synthetic rubber membranes, known for their elasticity and resilience, provide excellent waterproofing and UV resistance. Modified bitumen combines traditional asphalt with polymer additives, enhancing flexibility and strength, making it suitable for various climates. Each type caters to specific roofing needs, balancing performance, longevity, and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
