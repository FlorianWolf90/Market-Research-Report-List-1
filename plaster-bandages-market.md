<p><p>The plaster bandages market encompasses two primary types: glass fiber bandages and polyester fiber bandages. Glass fiber bandages are lightweight, strong, and water-resistant, making them ideal for orthopedic applications. They cure quickly and provide excellent support for fractures. In contrast, polyester fiber bandages are more flexible and offer good conformability, making them suitable for various applications, including wound care and orthotics. Both types are essential for ensuring effective immobilization and protection in clinical and rehabilitation settings.</p></p>
