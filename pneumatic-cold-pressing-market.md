<p><p>The pneumatic cold pressing market is categorized into three main types: single-stage, dual-stage, and multistage. </p><p>Single-stage pressing involves one compression cycle to achieve the desired pressure, suitable for simpler applications. Dual-stage pressing utilizes two compression cycles, allowing for higher efficiency and better material handling. Multistage pressing involves multiple cycles, enhancing the extraction of materials and achieving superior quality. Each type caters to different industrial needs, with variations in complexity, efficiency, and output quality.</p></p>
