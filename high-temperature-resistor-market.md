<p><p>The high-temperature resistor market includes various configurations, primarily single-in-line (SIL) and dual-in-line (DIL) types. SIL resistors feature a single row of terminals, allowing for compact designs and easy integration in electronic circuits. In contrast, DIL resistors have two parallel rows of pins, providing more connection options and stability, making them suitable for a wider range of applications. Both types are critical in high-temperature environments, ensuring reliable performance in automotive, aerospace, and industrial sectors.</p></p>
