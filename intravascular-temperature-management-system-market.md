<p><ul><li>Intravascular Warming</li><li>Intravascular Cooling</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Intravascular Temperature Management System market encompasses two primary types: intravascular warming and intravascular cooling. Intravascular warming involves the use of specialized devices to elevate a patient's core temperature, often during surgeries or in trauma care to prevent hypothermia. In contrast, intravascular cooling is employed to lower body temperature, particularly in cases of cardiac arrest or heat stroke, to protect vital organs. Both technologies are crucial in enhancing patient outcomes by effectively managing body temperature in clinical settings.</p></p>
