<p><p>The anti-microbial filter market comprises three key types: </p><p>1. **Non-Woven Fiber Filter**: These filters utilize a random arrangement of fibers that trap particles and microbes, offering effective filtration through layers of densely packed fibers. </p><p>2. **Nano Fiber Filter**: With ultra-fine fibers, nano fiber filters enhance filtration efficiency by capturing smaller particles and pathogens, making them suitable for advanced applications.</p><p>3. **Porous Polymeric Filter**: These filters feature a structured polymer matrix with adjustable pore sizes, providing tailored filtration while promoting air and liquid flow, ideal for various industrial uses.</p></p>
