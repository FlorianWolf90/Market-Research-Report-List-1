<p><strong>The Medical Cold Plasma Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Atmospheric Cold Plasma</li><li>Low-pressure Cold Plasma</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical cold plasma market is divided into two main types: atmospheric cold plasma and low-pressure cold plasma. Atmospheric cold plasma operates at normal atmospheric pressure and is primarily used for surface decontamination, wound healing, and sterilization due to its ability to produce reactive species that promote healing. In contrast, low-pressure cold plasma is generated in a vacuum and is commonly used in applications like plasma-assisted surgery and tissue engineering, where precise control and deeper tissue penetration are critical.</p></p>
