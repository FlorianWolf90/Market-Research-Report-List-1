<p><p>A Simultaneous Thermal Analyzer (STA) is an advanced instrument used to study the thermal properties of materials by combining Thermogravimetric Analysis (TGA) and Differential Thermal Analysis (DTA) in a single system. STA measures changes in mass and temperature simultaneously, allowing for a comprehensive understanding of material behavior under varying thermal conditions. It is widely employed in industries such as pharmaceuticals, materials science, and polymers to assess stability, composition, and decomposition processes.</p><p>The STA market is witnessing significant growth, fueled by increasing demand for materials characterization across various sectors, including aerospace, automotive, and consumer goods.
