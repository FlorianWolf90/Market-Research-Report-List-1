<p><strong>The Marine Emission Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Extractive CEMs</li><li>In-Situ CEMs</li><li>Portable CEMs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The marine emission sensors market is categorized into three main types: Extractive Continuous Emission Monitors (CEMs), In-Situ CEMs, and Portable CEMs. Extractive CEMs collect gas samples from the environment and analyze them in a laboratory setup, providing accurate and detailed data. In-Situ CEMs measure emissions directly in the location without sample extraction, offering real-time monitoring. Portable CEMs are handheld devices designed for on-the-go measurements, ideal for quick assessments and flexibility in various marine environments.</p></p>
