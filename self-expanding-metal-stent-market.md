<p><p>The self-expanding metal stent market is categorized into fully covered, partially covered, and uncovered types. Fully covered stents have a complete coating that prevents tissue ingrowth, making them suitable for specific anatomical applications. Partially covered stents feature a combination of covered and uncovered sections, offering flexibility for use in varying conditions. Uncovered stents, lacking any coating, allow for tissue attachment and are often employed in cases where permanent support is needed, facilitating natural tissue integration. Each type serves different clinical needs.</p></p>
