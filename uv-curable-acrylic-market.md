<p><p>UV curable acrylic refers to a type of acrylic resin that hardens or cures when exposed to ultraviolet (UV) light. Unlike traditional curing methods, UV curing eliminates the need for solvents, reducing VOC emissions and energy consumption.</p><p>The UV curable acrylic market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing demand across industries such as automotive, electronics, packaging, and healthcare.
