<p><p>The firefighting alarm sensors market comprises various types of detection technologies, each serving a unique purpose. Flame sensors detect the infrared or ultraviolet radiation emitted by fire, providing rapid response. Smoke sensors identify particulate matter from combustion, signaling the presence of fire early on. Heat sensors monitor temperature changes, activating alarms when thresholds are exceeded. Light sensors utilize changes in light levels to detect fire. Together, these technologies enhance safety and improve fire response effectiveness in various environments.</p></p>
