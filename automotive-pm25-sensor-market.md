<p><ul><li>Single Channel</li><li>Dual Channel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive PM2.5 sensor market is segmented into single-channel and dual-channel types. Single-channel sensors measure particulate matter levels using one detection pathway, making them simpler and cost-effective for basic applications. In contrast, dual-channel sensors utilize two separate pathways, enhancing accuracy and enabling differentiation between PM2.5 sources. This advanced capability allows for better monitoring of air quality, particularly in urban environments. The choice between these sensor types depends on the specific requirements of vehicle manufacturers and regulatory standards.</p></p>
