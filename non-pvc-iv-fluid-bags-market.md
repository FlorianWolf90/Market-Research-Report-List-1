<p><p>The Non-PVC IV Fluid Bags market comprises two main types: single-chambered and multi-chambered bags. Single-chambered bags contain one type of fluid, allowing for straightforward administration of medications or nutrients. In contrast, multi-chambered bags house multiple compartments, enabling the combined storage of different solutions that can be mixed prior to administration. This versatility reduces preparation time and enhances patient safety by minimizing the risk of contamination, making both types essential in healthcare settings for intravenous therapies.</p></p>
