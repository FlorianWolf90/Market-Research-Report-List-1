<p><ul><li>Active Tags</li><li>Passive Tags</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Wireless Sensor Tags market is categorized into two main types: active tags and passive tags. Active tags are equipped with their own power source, allowing for continuous transmission of data over long distances, making them suitable for real-time tracking applications. In contrast, passive tags rely on external energy sources, such as RFID readers, to operate. They are generally more cost-effective and have limited range but are ideal for scenarios where continuous monitoring is not required.</p></p>
