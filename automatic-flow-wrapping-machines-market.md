<p><p>Automatic flow wrapping machines are categorized into two main market types: horizontal and vertical. Horizontal flow wrapping machines operate by placing products horizontally on a conveyor, wrapping them with film in a continuous flow, ideal for flat or rectangular items. In contrast, vertical flow wrapping machines function by packaging products in an upright position, suitable for taller or irregularly shaped items. Both types enhance efficiency in packaging processes, catering to different product dimensions and production requirements across various industries.</p></p>
