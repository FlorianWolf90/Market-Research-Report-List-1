<p><strong>The Photoelectric Micro Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Reflective Photoelectric Micro Sensor</li><li>Diffuse Photoelectric Micro Sensor</li><li>Through Beam Photoelectric Micro Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The photoelectric micro sensor market comprises three main types: reflective, diffuse, and through beam sensors. Reflective photoelectric sensors detect objects by emitting a beam of light that reflects off the target back to the sensor. Diffuse sensors operate similarly but use scattered light, allowing them to detect nearby objects without a reflective surface. Through beam sensors consist of a light emitter and receiver positioned opposite each other, detecting objects that interrupt the light beam. Each type serves distinct automation and sensing applications.</p></p>
