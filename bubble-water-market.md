<p><p>Bubble water refers to carbonated beverages that are available in two main types: natural gas bubble water and artificial aerated water. Natural gas bubble water is sourced from mineral springs where carbon dioxide is naturally present, offering unique minerals and health benefits. On the other hand, artificial aerated water is produced by injecting carbon dioxide into still water, creating bubbles and a fizzy sensation. Both types cater to different consumer preferences, with natural options often perceived as healthier and more authentic.</p></p>
