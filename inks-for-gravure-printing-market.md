<p><p>The gravure printing market features three primary ink types: water-based inks, solvent-based inks, and UV-cured inks. Water-based inks are environmentally friendly, offering quick drying times and low toxicity. Solvent-based inks, characterized by their high durability and vibrant colors, are widely used for flexible packaging but can have higher VOC emissions. UV-cured inks utilize ultraviolet light for fast curing, resulting in high gloss and excellent adhesion. Each type serves unique applications, influencing quality, sustainability, and production efficiency.</p></p>
