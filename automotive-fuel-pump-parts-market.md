<p><p>The automotive fuel pump parts market encompasses several key types, including driven gears, driven shafts, driving shaft thrust bearings, transmission gears, and others. Driven gears facilitate power transfer from the engine, while driven shafts are essential for transmitting rotational motion. Driving shaft thrust bearings support axial loads, ensuring smooth operation. Transmission gears play a critical role in adjusting pump speeds based on engine demands. Other components may include various seals and housings, contributing to the overall functionality of fuel pumps in vehicles.</p></p>
