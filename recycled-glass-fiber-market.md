<p><p>The recycled glass fiber market encompasses various types based on processing methods. The mechanical method involves crushing and shredding glass fibers to create raw material for new products, ensuring energy efficiency. The calcination method involves heating glass fibers to high temperatures to remove organic materials, resulting in high-purity glass suitable for specialized applications. Other methods may include chemical treatments or hybrid techniques that combine mechanical and thermal processes, enhancing the versatility and quality of recycled glass fiber for industrial use.</p></p>
