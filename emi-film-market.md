<p><p>The EMI film market encompasses two primary types: metal-mesh films and coating films. Metal-mesh films utilize a grid of conductive metal, offering effective electromagnetic interference (EMI) shielding while maintaining transparency and flexibility. Coating films, on the other hand, are applied as a layer over surfaces, providing EMI protection without significantly altering aesthetics. Both types are essential in various industries, including electronics and automotive, where shielding against electromagnetic radiation is crucial for device performance and safety.</p></p>
