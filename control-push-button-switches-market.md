<p><p>Control push-button switches are essential components in industrial and commercial applications, categorized primarily into three types: mushroom, rotary, and other variants. Mushroom switches feature a large, dome-shaped button designed for emergency stops, offering quick access in critical situations. Rotary switches, on the other hand, allow users to control functions by rotating a knob, enabling versatile settings. Other types include toggle switches and momentary push buttons, which cater to specific operational needs, enhancing functionality and user control across various environments.</p></p>
