<p><p>The Stable Isotope Analyzer market is primarily divided into two types: Stable Isotope Ratio Mass Spectrometry (IRMS) and laser-based analyzers. In contrast, laser-based analyzers employ advanced laser technology to quickly and efficiently determine isotopic ratios, making them suitable for field applications and rapid analysis. Each type serves distinct needs in research, industrial applications, and environmental monitoring.</p></p>
