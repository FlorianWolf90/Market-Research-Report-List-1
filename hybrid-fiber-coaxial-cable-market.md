<p><p>The Hybrid Fiber-Coaxial (HFC) cable market integrates both fiber and coaxial components, optimizing bandwidth and transmission efficiency for telecommunications and internet services. In this context, single-mode fiber features a narrow core, allowing for longer distances and higher data rates, making it ideal for backbone connections. Conversely, multimode fiber has a larger core, facilitating shorter distances and higher data capacity for local area networks. Together, these fiber types enhance HFC networks, supporting a wide range of communication applications.</p></p>
